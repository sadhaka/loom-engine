Loom Engine - Perf Bench

Browser-side bench runner. Drives the standardized scenarios in tools/perf-suite.ts against the engine built at ../dist/index.js. Heap stats are unavailable in the browser; that's a runtime limit, not a bug. Set ?frames=900&warmup=60&scenarios=sprite-scaling,ecs-iteration&label=iPhone%20SE&backend=canvas2d via querystring to scope the run.

Real-device tip: serve tools/ over your LAN (npx serve tools/), open this page on the device under test, keep the tab foregrounded, and disable low-power mode. Backgrounded tabs are throttled to ~1fps and will read as a 1000ms+ frame time which is the throttling budget, not the engine's.
