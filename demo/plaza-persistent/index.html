Loom Engine - Plaza Persistent

The plaza that remembers: persistence + partial sync, proven in one seeded run. Twelve villagers across four regions live two epochs on an HMAC event chain, the world suspends into a sealed bundle, resumes through full chain verification plus twelve deterministic offline epochs, then a client pulls ONLY the regions that changed and proves the recombined Merkle root.
